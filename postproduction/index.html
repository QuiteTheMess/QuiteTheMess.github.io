Post-Production

Here is a 5 minute showreel which showcases my ability in Audio Post-Production, predominantly as a Sound Designer. I have also worked as a Dialogue + Music Editor, and as a Mixer on several projects, including Alfie (2025), Helen (2025), and Chewing Gum Ad (2025) which are featured in the showreel. I have extensive experience working with Avid Pro Tools, Izotope RX 11, and creating SFX using both foley modulation and audio synthesis. I have some experience working with game engine middleware.

Below are some ambiences + sound effects I made for a 1 week game jam game called "How To Sleep", a short horror game themed after Playstation 1 era horror titles. I created the effects to sound as if from an actual Playstation 1 game, utilising Playstation 1 sound chip style reverbs and bitcrush.

The game is playable in browser at https://paceproxy.itch.io/how-to-sleep.

Keywords: Horror, PSX, PS1
